1. If the user named a dish, read its recipe file in this module; otherwise list the available recipes and ask which one.
2. Walk through the ingredients first, then the steps in order, one stage at a time.
3. Call out timing, temperatures, and any step where things commonly go wrong.
4. Stay practical and encouraging; never invent ingredients that are not in the recipe.
